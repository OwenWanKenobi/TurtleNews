Turtle News turns the playful spirit of the original Google Site into a scalable newsroom. The site covers serious turtle science, conservation, internet culture, product experiments like TurtleGPT, and the occasional strange dispatch from the wider shell universe.

The editorial format is intentionally simple: posts live in `_posts`, projects live in `_data/apps.yml`, and core pages are plain Markdown or HTML. That keeps the site fast, portable, and easy to publish on GitHub Pages.

## What We Cover

- Science, evolution, archaeology, and conservation
- Culture, entertainment, wellness, and internet trends
- Turtle technology, including TurtleGPT and related projects
- Events, submissions, newsletters, and community updates
